Lentil Lasagna

A plant-based twist on the classic Italian dish, replacing traditional ingredients like meat and cheese with layers of vegetables, tofu, or vegan cheese, all stacked between sheets of pasta and savory tomato sauce.

Shoyu Ramen

Shoyu ramen is a Japanese noodle soup featuring wheat noodles in a soy sauce-based broth, typically garnished with toppings like sliced green onions, seaweed, and occasionally, marinated bamboo shoots or a boiled egg. The savory broth complements the noodles and diverse toppings for a flavorful and satisfying dish.

Aloo Gobi

Aloo gobi is a North Indian dish featuring cauliflower (gobi) and potatoes (aloo) cooked together with aromatic spices, such as turmeric, cumin, and coriander, creating a flavorful and comforting vegetarian curry.
